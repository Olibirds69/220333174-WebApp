Baguio's Finest

Baguio Botanical Garden

Home to a vast array of plants, including a variety of flowering plants, trees and shrubs. You'll find everything from blooming azaleas to towering pine trees. Additionally, the gardens are home to a number of wildlife speacies, such as birds, that call Botanical garden as their home.

Burnham Park

an open green park at the heart of Baguio. The park's lush greenery and blossoming blooms make you forget you're in the heart of a densely populated metropolis. Burnham Park is easily accessible; it is located along Harrison Orad, just a short distance from Session Road, Baguio's main highway.

Wright Park

A wooded area in Baguio which became known for its horseback riding services for tourists. It was named after the American Governor Luke E. Wright and was established as a recreational area. It is situated near the Mansion.
